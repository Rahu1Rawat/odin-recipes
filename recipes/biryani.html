Biryani

Description

Biryani is a popular and flavorful dish that originated in South Asia, with roots in Indian, Pakistani, and Persian cuisines. It's a fragrant and aromatic rice dish typically prepared with a combination of long-grain Basmati rice, tender meat (such as chicken, lamb, beef, or goat), and a blend of carefully selected spices.
